Our Story

HerMatchUp was born from a simple yet powerful realization: women in STEM fields often face unique challenges that require understanding, support, and guidance from those who've walked similar paths.

As three computer science and engineering students studying in Kosovo and across Europe, we’ve experienced firsthand the challenges that come with pursuing a career in tech. We often found ourselves seeking mentors who could understand not just the technical demands of our field, but also the cultural, social, and personal challenges we faced.

That's when we decided to create something different, a platform that goes beyond traditional networking to create meaningful, lasting mentorship relationships based on shared experiences, goals, and values.

"We wanted to build the platform we wished we had when we started our journey in tech."

- The HerMatchUp Founders

Our Mission

To create a supportive ecosystem where women in STEM can find mentors, build meaningful relationships, and accelerate their career growth through personalized guidance and shared experiences.

Our Vision

A world where every woman in STEM has access to the mentorship, support, and opportunities she needs to thrive, innovate, and lead in her chosen field.

Our Values

Authenticity, inclusivity, empowerment, and community. We believe in creating genuine connections that foster both personal and professional growth.
